Our Company

PT. Konderatu Design Center is an interior design company established in 1989, renowned for offering various design, manufacturing, and contracting services specifically for the hotel, hospitality industry, and high-end residential markets. With its signature style, dedicated yet creative production staff, and savvy facilities. The company continues to provide good quality products in the hands of professional artisans by using beautiful rich materials from all over Indonesia and exceptional service being applied at every level handled by our experienced professional's team.

Become arespected name in the interior construction industry and located in Bali, that well known as a beautiful place globally, with nature and tradition. We are standing out for our attention to detail and providing consistent quality work. Specializes in interior construction, regardless of design, size, scope, and business area, and offers a complete and competent array of services to fulfill the most demanding and ambitious projects and taking on the most challenging interior projects.
